Replace IATF Youth Clubs image (iatf-youth-clubs.jpg)
====================================================

Option A – Run in terminal (replace the path with your chosen image):

  Copy-Item "C:\PATH\TO\YOUR\chosen-image.jpg" "C:\Users\burri\OneDrive\MY PORTFOLIO\public\iatf-youth-clubs.jpg" -Force

Option B – Use the script:

  1. Open public\copy-iatf-photo.ps1
  2. Change the line with $source to your image path, e.g.:
     $source = "C:\Users\burri\OneDrive\Pictures\Pictures\Saved Pictures\my-photo.jpg"
  3. In terminal:  cd "C:\Users\burri\OneDrive\MY PORTFOLIO\public"
  4. Run:  .\copy-iatf-photo.ps1

Option C – Manual replace:

  1. Delete or rename the current public\iatf-youth-clubs.jpg
  2. Copy your chosen image into the public folder
  3. Rename it to:  iatf-youth-clubs.jpg
