Project images (from your PC Pictures folder)

To use your own pictures:

1. Open your Pictures folder and find the image you want.
2. Copy the image (Ctrl+C or right-click > Copy).
3. Open this folder in File Explorer:  MY PORTFOLIO\public\
4. Paste the image here (Ctrl+V).
5. Rename the pasted file to the correct name:
   - Police Innovation card:  police-innovation.png
   - IATF Youth Clubs card:  iatf-youth-clubs.jpg

The portfolio will then show your image on the corresponding project card.
